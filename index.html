Sol Arena y Mar

It is the first song on the EP with the same name, an intro that expresses what it feels like to go enjoy the beach, what it means to detach yourself from any worry that one has, and what it means to meet someone and lose the fear of what that may happen, just follow what you feel. A dancehall inspired by projects like AfroLOVA'23, with characteristic guitars of the genre and synth that simulate marimbas that give a tropical vibe.

Maybe not all stories end with happy endings, but they leave us with good memories, the summer ends and many things go with it, but we will live remembering the good for a long time. As the last song, an afrobeat that follows the line of tropical sounds, generating a nocturnal atmosphere, an atmosphere of farewell, not only to the person in the story, but also to the listeners, ending the story of two people who may never meet again. seen, turning it into a summer memory.
